A Beginners Guide to Disc Golf

Before you decide to go disc golfing for the first time there are a few things to know. Even if you have disc golfed before, these could be helpful tips.

Attire as important, as your round will be a short hike. Shoes or boots that can handle being wet and/or muddy are advantageous but not necessary. Sunscreen is also important if the course is relatively open. Writing your name and phone number on your disc is important in case you lose it. Disc golf players will usually contact the person written on discs they find and try to arrange a way to return it. Disc color can seem unimportant, but losing a disc is easier than expected for most players. Frequently Asked Questions

Question #1 What are the rules with an out of bounds throw? The player gains one stroke and throws from the disc went out of bounds. If there is a designated drop zone, then the player throws from there and still gains a stroke.

Question #2 Who throws first after we throw our first throws? The player furthest away from the basket.
