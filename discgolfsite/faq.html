FAQ

Before you decide to go disc golfing for the first time there are a few things to know. Even if you have disc golfed before, these could be helpful tips.

Attire as important, as your round will be a short hike. Shoes or boots that can handle being wet and/or muddy are advantageous but not necessary. Sunscreen is also important if the course is relatively open. A bag to carry your discs in is extremely helpful, as you won't need to place your discs on the ground during every shot. Its is also extremely helpful to bring a towel with you to clean and dry your discs if necessary.

Disc color can seem unimportant, but losing a disc is easier than expected for most players. Writing your name and phone number on your disc is also important in case you lose it. Disc golf players will usually contact the person written on discs they find and try to arrange a way to return it.

FAQ

Question #1

What are the rules with an out of bounds throw?
A: The player gains one stroke and throws from the disc went out of bounds. If there is a designated drop zone, then the player throws from there and still gains a stroke.

Question #2

Who throws first after we throw our first throws?
A: The player furthest away from the basket.

Question #3

Where do you tbrow from after your throw?
A:Directly behind where the disc lies.
